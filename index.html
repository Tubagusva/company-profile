Intermediate

Intermediate refers to a mid-level stage of learning, ideal for individuals who have mastered the basics and are ready to build on that foundation, but are not yet at an advanced level. It serves as a crucial transition point for further skill development and deeper understanding.

Degree

A degree is an academic qualification awarded by universities or colleges, signifying that an individual has successfully completed a specific course of study. It serves as a formal recognition of expertise in a particular field and is often a key requirement for professional advancement and career opportunities.

LPost Graduation refers to advanced academic study undertaken after earning an undergraduate degree.
